Our Story

Painting Hobby Gal, a website brimming with vibrant hues and textured canvases, is the brainchild of Sarah Jenkins, a self-taught artist who rediscovered her passion for painting after years of working in a corporate job. Sarah's journey began with a simple watercolor set and a desire to escape the monotony of office life. Her contemporary style, characterized by bold strokes and an expressive use of color, quickly garnered attention within her local art community, leading to her first small exhibition at a neighborhood cafe. The website, launched shortly after, serves as both a dynamic portfolio showcasing her evolving artistic journey and an online shop where art enthusiasts from around the globe can purchase her original pieces. From abstract landscapes inspired by her travels through the rugged Scottish Highlands to striking portraits capturing the nuanced essence of human emotion, each painting tells a story, a fragment of Sarah's own experience translated onto canvas. Sarah diligently updates the site with new artwork, artist statements, and behind-the-scenes glimpses into her creative process, sharing the raw, unfiltered moments of inspiration and struggle. This transparency fosters a genuine connection with her growing audience, transforming them from mere viewers into a community of fellow art lovers.

Beyond the website, Painting Hobby Gal extends its reach across various social media platforms, creating a vibrant digital ecosystem for art enthusiasts. Sarah actively engages with her followers on Instagram (@artdesignbyari, sharing daily updates on her work in progress, from the initial sketch to the final brushstroke, studio setup tips, and even the occasional impromptu art challenge, inviting her followers to participate. Her Facebook page,contemporary painting, serves as a hub for announcements about upcoming online exhibitions, special promotions, and links to in-depth blog posts where she discusses her artistic inspirations, the techniques she employs, and the stories behind her most beloved pieces. Through these interconnected platforms, Sarah has cultivated a thriving online community, connecting with fellow artists, seasoned art collectors, and individuals who simply appreciate the beauty and power of contemporary art. Her digital presence is not just a showcase of her work, but a testament to the power of art to connect people across geographical and cultural boundaries, creating a space for dialogue, inspiration, and shared appreciation.
